Welcome to this page

"Suits Addition" is a website created by and for passionate fans of the iconic series "Suits." Our motivation is to channel the overwhelming enthusiasm we feel for this incredible TV production and provide the community with a dedicated space to share, explore, and celebrate everything related to the series.


At the core of our motivation is an admiration for the talented actors who have brought the unforgettable characters of "Suits" to life. From Gabriel Macht's compelling portrayal of Harvey Specter to Meghan Markle's charismatic presence as Rachel Zane, each cast member has made an exceptional contribution to the success of the series. "Suits Addition" seeks to pay homage to their outstanding work and provide fans with a platform where they can express their gratitude and admiration.


The success of "Suits" has transcended the screens, becoming a cultural phenomenon. We are inspired by the passionate community of followers that has grown around the series, and our website aims to further strengthen those bonds. From episode analyses to cast news, "Suits Addition" is designed to be the ultimate destination where fans can dive into the exciting universe of "Suits" and share their love for this legally captivating story.



Join us and let's celebrate together the greatness of "Suits" in all its facets!
